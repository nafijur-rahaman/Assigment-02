Standard Curriculum

Kinder (3-6) Years

Our kindergarten program focuses on developing social, emotional, and cognitive skills through play-based learning.

Elementary School

Our elementary school curriculum includes a strong foundation in reading, writing, math, and science, along with arts and physical education.

Middle (10-16) Years

Our middle school program prepares students for high school with advanced courses in various subjects and opportunities for extracurricular activities.
